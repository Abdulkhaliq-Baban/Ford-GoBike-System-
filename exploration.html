The dataset has around 85,000 rides in January 2018 & has information for each trip as list:

  • duration_sec
  • start_time
  • end_time
  • bike_id
  • user_type
  • bike_share_for_all_trip
  • start_time_hour
  • start_time_day
  • start_time_weekday
  • start_time_month_name
  • start_time_month
